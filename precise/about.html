About US

We are a young and flexible hydraulic service company from Bengaluru, India focusing on the designing & manufacturing custom hydraulic systems, cylinders, manifold block and distribution of hydraulic components from domestic and international manufacturers. Our strength is a high added value in service, customer satisfaction and purchase bundling.

We provide customer driven solutions offering the most compact, power dense products available anywhere in the world. PFD is thoroughly involved in the customer’s design process and we design custom made manifolds, cylinders and entire hydraulic systems to fit as per customer requirements which are all manufactured in-house.

We would be pleased to quote all your hydraulic requirements and are confident that you will find PRECISE FLUID DYNAMICS a competitive quality source for all your hydraulic component needs.

Our Vision

The vision of Precise fluid dynamics is to continue the heritage of our Company and be a premier Indian manufacturer of top quality products.
